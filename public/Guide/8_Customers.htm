Customers

A Customer record captures all of the relevant information related to an individual or group, including contact details, eligibility and required accommodations.

Before a Trip can be scheduled for a Customer, an account must be created for that Customer to capture the relevant information.

The Customers tab displays a list of the defined Customers, and allows you to create new Customers, and update existing Customers.

Search Customers

To search for a specific Customer:

1.      Enter the search text in the "Search" textbox above the table, and click Search.

2.      The table is updated to display only those Customers that match the search text.

Display a Customer

To view a Customer:

1.      Click the Customer name hyperlink in the table.

2.      The details of the selected Customer will be displayed. To return to the Customers Summary page, click Back.

When viewing a Customer, you can create a new Trip for the Customer by clicking New Trip.

New Customer

To create a new Customer, either as an individual or a group:

1.      Click either New Customer or New Group at the top-right of the page, as appropriate.

2.      A blank Customer details page is displayed:

3.      Enter all of the required information, and click Save.

4.      Assuming all of the entered Run information is validated, the Run will be created.

A mailing address must be defined for the Customer.

Edit Customer

To edit an existing Customer:

1.      View the details of the existing Customer.

2.      Click Edit at the top-right of the page.

3.      The Customer details will be displayed in "Edit Mode" allowing the values to be edited.

4.      Make the appropriate edits, and click Update at the top-right of the page.

5.      Assuming all of the edits are validated, the Customer will be updated.

Merge Customers

For various reasons, multiple records for the same Customer may be created. For example, the same Customer makes separate requests to multiple Providers who each create a Customer record. These multiple records can be merged together to create a single record to which all of the associated Trips are attached. To merge Customer records:

1.      View the details of the one of the records for the Customer.

2.      Click Merge at the top-right of the page. A dialog is displayed requesting the name of the other Customer record:

3.      Start entering the name, and select the correct Customer name when it appears in the dropdown.

4.      Click Confirm to merge the two Customer records.

Inactivate a Customer

Occasionally, a Customer may be restricted from requesting Trips (e.g., they have a recent history of no shows). As such, a Customer may be temporarily placed on the inactive list:

1.      View the details of the Customer.

2.      Click Inactivate at the top-right of the page.

3.      A confirmation dialog is displayed asking you to provide a reason for the inactivation:

4.      Enter the reason, and click Inactive to inactivate the Customer.

While a Customer is inactive, they cannot request Trips.

Activate a Customer

To re-activate an inactive Customer:

1.      View the details of the Customer.

2.      Click Activate at the top-right of the page.
